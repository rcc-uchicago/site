General users must be associated with a principal investigator (PI), who has an existing PI account. General user accounts allow access to the resources allocated to the associated PI. After submitting this form, the listed PI must approve the account request before the new general user account can be activated and added to their group.

By submitting this form you agree to the RCC User Policy.

 

*indicates a required field

The RCC uses the University of Chicago CNetID to authenticate users on its systems. If you already have a CNetID please provide it below. If not, the RCC can request one for you. Please provide your date of birth, title, department, and university, which will be used to uniquely identify your request.
